# Esports Tournament Prediction

## When to Use

- Predicting League of Legends tournament or regional league outcomes
- Forecasting Dota 2 Major/TI results
- Predicting CS2 Major outcomes
- Any competitive gaming tournament winner or match result prediction

## Data Sources

- **Liquipedia**: Comprehensive esports wiki with results, rosters, and tournament info
- **Gol.gg / Oracle's Elixir**: Advanced League of Legends statistics
- **HLTV.org**: CS2 team rankings and match statistics  
- **DotaBuff / OpenDota**: Dota 2 statistics
- **Betting odds**: Esports-specialized bookmakers (Pinnacle, Betway)
- **Prediction markets**: Manifold for major events

## Methodology

### Tournament Winner Predictions
1. **Check betting odds** — esports odds are reasonably efficient for top-tier events
2. **Regional strength** — historically, LCK (Korea) and LPL (China) dominate LoL Worlds
3. **Recent form** — domestic league results and head-to-head records
4. **Roster changes** — new players need time to integrate; roster stability is predictive
5. **Patch meta** — game balance changes before tournaments affect team strengths

### Regional Strength Priors (League of Legends)
- **LCK** (Korea): Most consistent region, 6+ Worlds titles. Favored for any international event.
- **LPL** (China): Second strongest, won multiple Worlds. Size of league means high variance.
- **LEC** (Europe): Occasional dark horse, rarely wins international events. 
- **LCS** (NA): Historically weakest major region for international events.
- **Other regions** (CBLOL, LCP, etc.): Rarely advance past group stage internationally.

## Prediction Tips

- For "which region will the champion come from?", LCK has been the safest bet historically (~50% of Worlds titles)
- LPL has improved significantly and is close to 50/50 with LCK in recent years
- Non-LCK/LPL regions winning a major international LoL event is very rare (<10%)
- In CS2, the skill gap between top teams is smaller — upsets are more common (~30% for top-8 vs top-8)
- Meta shifts from patches can invalidate recent form data — check patch timing
- Online results don't perfectly predict LAN performance

## Example Predictions

**Input**: "Which region will the 2026 First Stand Tournament champion come from?"
**Approach**: Check which teams qualified from each region, betting odds, recent international results. LCK or LPL is the strong default. LCK+LPL combined probability is typically 75-85%.
